Games

Funopolis pals believe play is an important part of the learning process. Come play the interactive games such as freestanding Tic-Tac-Toes and Jumpy ball with other children as a fun way to learn things, make new friends, and explore the world around you. The lessons you can learn and abilities you can develop from such activities will be priceless and worthwhile.
